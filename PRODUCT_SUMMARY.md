# First Choice Transportation – Product Summary

**GPS-verified driver timesheets for transportation companies**

---

## The problem

Transportation companies struggle with accurate driver timesheets. Manual time entries are easy to inflate, and basic GPS trackers only show where someone was—not whether they were actually working. Companies overpay for idle time, face compliance risk, and spend hours reconciling timesheets.

---

## Who it’s for

- **Transportation companies** (non-emergency medical transport, shuttle, paratransit)
- **Operations managers** who need reliable driver hours
- **Owners** who want to reduce payroll leakage and compliance risk

---

## How it works

Drivers clock in on their phone. The app tracks their location and movement during the shift. When they finish their last dropoff, they tap “Final Dropoff.” The app estimates travel time back to their base (home or office) and automatically calculates verified hours from clock-in to that return time. No manual clock-out. Admins see drivers, weekly totals, and shift details—including shifts that were flagged for review.

---

## Why it matters

- **Accuracy:** Hours are based on movement and route data, not manual edits.
- **Trust:** Drivers and admins both see the same verified hours.
- **Efficiency:** No manual timesheet entry or reconciliation.
- **Compliance:** A clear record of when and where drivers worked.

---

## What makes it different

Most GPS time trackers only log location. First Choice Transportation:

- **Verifies work time** using movement and route logic, not just presence.
- **Auto-completes shifts** using estimated return-to-base time instead of manual clock-out.
- **Flags suspicious patterns** (e.g. no movement at start, long idle periods, unusually long shifts) for admin review.
- **Keeps it simple** for drivers: clock in, drive, tap Final Dropoff.

It’s built for transportation workflows, not generic field workers.
